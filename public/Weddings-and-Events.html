Weddings and Events


We want to capture and convey the moments of
joy by surprising and delighting our customers by
creating beautiful designs that celebrate lifes
memorable occasions.

Weddings

We approach each project with
passion, a personal touch and
creativity. The aesthetic, defined by
tailored precision and lush femininity,
is a contemporary take on classic
French Style. Whether it’s a bridal
bouquet or a grand scale production,
LA ROSE composition inspire a sense
of discovery, delight, surprise, and
romance.

Celebrations

Celebrating special moments
together with family and friends is
one of the most important things
in life. Enjoy these moments with
gorgeous flowers or plants to
personalize the ambiance and party
vibe. Whether it’s an anniversary,
birthday or celebrating a goal that
you have achieved - mark that
moment with a beautifully hand-
crafted arrangement from LA ROSE.

Events

LA ROSE can direct and produce each
event, curating everything from
show-stopping florals, twinkling
roses constellation to exquisite
handling installations that transform a
space. All elements are seamlessly
integrated, creating a sumptuous,
verdant visual vocabulary that
captures each occasion’s theme, and
beautifully plays to all of the senses.
