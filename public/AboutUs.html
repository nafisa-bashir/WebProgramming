About Us

LA ROSE is inspired by sense of place and season, which
is fundamental for our floral designs. The process of
creating a floral arrangement involves many of the
same principles that go into making fine art. Balancing
colour with texture, seasonality, foliage and foraged
elements makes every arrangement unique piece of
art.


We are committed to personalizing your experience by
offering more than just flowers. Each design has its
individual touch depending on seasonality, occasion and
your individual preference.


Our shop in DIFC serves as a beautifully curated retail
boutique, which allows for a truly delightful experience
for the senses that we hope to share with you.
